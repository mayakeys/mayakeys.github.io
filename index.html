Maya Keys

I'm a 3rd year undergraduate student at Caltech, studying Computer Science with minor in Robotics. I’m interested in computer vision and robotics with a focus on embodied perception, multimodal sensing, and visual reasoning. I’m drawn to problems where agents must build 3D world models of their environment from multimodal sensory input and use them for real-time reasoning and decision-making.

I am currently working on MiniEELS, a snake-like, high-DOF robotic platform inspired by JPL’s EELS for autonomous navigation in construction sites.
